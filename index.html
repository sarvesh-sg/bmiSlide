Know your Body Mass Index

The body mass index (BMI), or Quetelet index, is a measure of relative weight based on an individual's mass and height.

The BMI is used in a wide variety of contexts as a simple method to assess how much an individual's body weight departs from what is normal or desirable for a person of his or her height.

The source is the wikipedia entry for BMI Sample BMI calculation:

weight <- 74

Age Factor:

For children (classified as someone below 20 yrs),

BMI is calculated w.r.t standard deviation of a sample population.

Overweight: greater than 1 Standard Deviation Obesity: greater than 2 Standard Deviation Thinness: less than -1 Standard Deviation Severe thinness: less than -2 Standard Deviation
